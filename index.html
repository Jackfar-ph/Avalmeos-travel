About Philippines

With over 7,000 islands that make up the archipelago of the Philippines, a constant challenge for travelers is to figure out where to explore first. One thing's for sure — there's something to see for every type of traveler.

History buffs can make their way to colonial Spanish heritage sites in Old Manila, nestled in the south of Luzon - one of the Philippines' three major islands. Cliff jumping and other exciting water sports await adrenaline junkies in Puerto Princesa, and hardcore beach babies will have their fill of sand between their toes with a visit to any of Iloilo's homey towns. With so much to see and do, the Philippines can easily become your next favorite tropical paradise.
